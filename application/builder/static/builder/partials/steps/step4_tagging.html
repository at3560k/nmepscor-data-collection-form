NMEPSCoR Data Documentation Tool: Keywords

Thematic and/or spatial keywords help make your data discoverable in the NM EPSCoR data portal. These can be terms that are common to your research area to describe the larger context of your data (Solar Energy) as well as more specific terms to highlight these particular data files (Polycrystalline thin-films).



Tagging

Please include a list of terms to describe the collection. Include as many terms as you’d like, separating each term with a comma. Examples include “Precipitation, Temperature, Relative Humidity, Weather” for a set of weather station datasets.

If your data includes a spatial component, please include a list of placenames, separated by a comma. Please enter placenames if you are providing a report about a particular watershed or metro area (Socorro or Pecos River) or a general location for your study area (Middle Rio Grande or Lincoln County). Consider going from more to less specific locations, for example "Albuquerque, Bernalillo County, Middle Rio Grande Watershed, New Mexico." to
